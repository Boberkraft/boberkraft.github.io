About me

Why such weird languages?

At first, I used PHP/Js for creating demo websites containing simple scripts and applications. I started with web technologies because they provide better feedback and fast results - then I moved on to backend (Python, Haxe, C++, Common Lisp) for learning purposes

Thanks to That I'm easily able to comprehend complex recursion, generators, and metaprogramming. I can differentiate the good algorithm from the bad algorithm + I know when to stop worrying about it one in order to stop micro-optimization from happening.

I'm very good at googling and finding information on the internet. No one has to supervise me. I respect both my own and people's time (time is money).

I used C++ when I was learning about electrics and electronics, mainly for programming my Arduino. Using it, I have created a binary clock, Simon says game, and a led strip controlled by music from a computer.

Why me?

I'm most satisfied when I'm challenged.

I was always the top student in my Secondary school and graduated with honors (as one of two). Besides learning from school, I learned to program out of my own will at home. I can spend hours and days in front of a computer screen because it gives me the power to create - and that's my true passion.
